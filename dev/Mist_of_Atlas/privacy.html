Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 4, 2026

At Mist of Atlas: World of Fog, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you use our application and website.

1. Data We Collect

To provide the core "Fog-of-War" experience, we collect and process location data from your device. This data is used to reveal territory on your personal atlas and calculate exploration metrics.

2. How We Use Your Data

Location data is processed locally on your device or transmitted to our servers to sync your map across devices and enable shared guild maps. We do not sell your location history to third parties.

3. Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to your data, request correction, or request erasure of your personal data at any time through the app settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@bobsnadenica.com.
