How signals work

1 day

Short momentum

20-day trend, five-session momentum, RSI, MACD, and Bollinger position.

1 month

Medium trend

50-day trend, 20/50-day alignment, one-month return, MACD, and regime.

1 year

Long trend

200-day trend, 50/200-day alignment, one-year return, regime, and distance from the yearly high.
