# Brand voice & standing instructions

This file is part of AIPost247's "memory". Everything here is fed to the AI as
context before it writes each post. Edit it freely — plain Markdown/text.

Describe things like:

- **Who you are:** what the Page/brand is about.
- **Audience:** who you are talking to.
- **Tone:** e.g. friendly, professional, witty, inspirational.
- **Topics to cover:** product tips, industry news, behind-the-scenes, etc.
- **Things to avoid:** banned topics, competitors, over-promising, etc.
- **Call to action:** what you want readers to do (visit site, comment, share).

## Example (replace this)

We are a small specialty coffee roaster. We talk to home-brewing enthusiasts in
a warm, knowledgeable, slightly playful tone. We share brewing tips, origin
stories, and seasonal offers. Avoid hard-selling. End most posts with a gentle
invitation to reply or visit the shop. Keep it concise and skimmable.
