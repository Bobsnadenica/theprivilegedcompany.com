AIPost247 · Setup Guide

Connect your Facebook Page

Do this one-time setup (about 3–5 minutes), and then everything runs locally from the script — it logs you in, finds your Page, writes posts with AI, and publishes them on your schedule.

⏱ One-time · ~3–5 min 🔒 Credentials stay on your computer ✅ No App Review for your own Pages
Good to know. You manage your own Pages in the app's default Development mode — no App Review and no Business Verification required.
Why is this needed? Facebook only lets a program post to a Page through a registered Meta app — the same reason tools like Buffer or Hootsuite ask you to “connect Facebook”. You create that app once; after that the script handles the rest on your machine.

1Create a Facebook developer account

Free and instant — uses your normal Facebook login.

  1. Open developers.facebook.com.
  2. Click Get Started (top-right) and sign in with your Facebook account.
  3. Accept the developer terms and confirm your email or phone if prompted.

2Create an app

A container that lets the script talk to your Page.

  1. Go to developers.facebook.com/apps → Create app.
  2. If asked what you want to build, choose Other → Next.
  3. Select app type Business → Next.
  4. Enter an App name (anything, e.g. My Page Poster) and your contact email → Create app (re-enter your password if asked).

3Add Facebook Login & the redirect URI

This lets the browser hand the login safely back to the script.

  1. On the app dashboard, open Add products (or Use cases) and add Facebook Login for Business (or Facebook Login) → Set up.
  2. Open that product's Settings.
  3. In Valid OAuth Redirect URIs, paste this exactly and click Save: 
    http://localhost:8723/
    It must be http (not https), port 8723, with the trailing slash. A wrong value causes a “URL Blocked” error during login.

4Copy your App ID & App Secret

The two values the script asks for — entered once.

  1. In the left menu, open App settings → Basic.
  2. Copy the App ID shown at the top.
  3. Next to App Secret, click Show (re-enter your password) and copy it.

5Run the script — fully local from here

Paste the two values once, log in, pick your Page, set a schedule.

  1. In a terminal: 
    cd dev/aipost247
./run.sh setup        # Windows: run.bat setup
  2. Content generator → choose Gemini and log in with Google (no API key).
  3. Facebook → choose [1] Connect with Facebook, paste your App ID and App Secret. A browser opens → approve → pick your Page from the list. The script stores a long-lived Page token for you.
  4. Schedule → type 2 to publish every 2 hours.
  5. Go live (and keep it running in the background): 
    ./run.sh run
# background (survives closing the terminal):
nohup ./run.sh run > aipost247.out 2>&1 &

Tip: run ./run.sh generate first to preview a post without publishing.

Permissions requested

pages_show_list, pages_read_engagement, pages_manage_posts — only enough to list your Pages and publish to the one you choose. In Development mode these work for your own Pages with no review.

Troubleshooting

What you seeFix
“URL Blocked” / “redirect_uri isn't allowed”The redirect URI isn't saved. Add http://localhost:8723/ exactly in Facebook Login → Settings, then Save.
Browser login never returns / times outSame cause — the redirect URI must match exactly.
Your Page isn't in the listYou must be an admin of the Page and grant the permissions during login.
“Invalid or expired token” later onRe-run ./run.sh setup and log in again.
Port 8723 already in useClose whatever is using it and retry.

Security

Your App Secret and Page token live only in dev/aipost247/.env (permissions 600, gitignored). They are never committed, uploaded, or shared. Post text goes only to Google (Gemini); tokens go only to Facebook. The whole app runs on your machine — nothing is hosted by anyone else.
