Legal Framework

Terms of Engagement.

Plain-language terms for working with ThePrivilegedCompany. This is a starting draft — please review it with your own legal advisor and complete the bracketed details before you rely on it.

01

About these terms

These terms cover work carried out by ThePrivilegedCompany ("we", "us") for a client ("you"). They apply alongside any specific proposal or written agreement we sign with you. Where a signed proposal and these terms disagree, the signed proposal wins. You can reach us at contactus@theprivilegedcompany.com.

02

Scope of work

We agree the scope of each project in writing before work starts. Anything outside that agreed scope is a separate piece of work that we quote and confirm before doing it. We will not add billable work by surprise.

03

Quotes, fees and payment

We provide a clear plan and price before any work begins — we do not quote blind. Fixed-price work is invoiced as set out in the proposal; ongoing work is invoiced periodically. One-to-one tech training is 100 euro per session. Invoices are due within the period stated on the invoice. We may pause work on overdue accounts after giving you notice.

04

Timelines

Any dates we give are good-faith estimates. Delivery depends on you providing access, content, approvals, and feedback in reasonable time. We will tell you promptly if something puts a timeline at risk.

05

Your responsibilities

You agree to give us the access, information, and materials we need, to make sure you have the rights to any content you provide, and to respond to questions and approvals in reasonable time so the work can move forward.

06

Confidentiality

Each side keeps the other's non-public information confidential and uses it only for the project. We are happy to sign a mutual NDA. We do not publish your name or project details in our own marketing without your written consent.

07

Intellectual property

Once you have paid in full for a piece of work, the deliverables created specifically for you become yours. We keep ownership of our pre-existing tools, libraries, and general know-how, and we may reuse that know-how on other projects. Third-party and open-source components stay under their own licences.

08

Third-party services

Projects often rely on third-party services — for example hosting, cloud providers, or the form service that delivers your contact messages. Those services are governed by their own terms, and we are not responsible for their availability or actions.

09

Warranties and liability

We carry out our work with reasonable skill and care. We do not guarantee specific business outcomes such as revenue, search rankings, or third-party approvals, because those depend on factors outside our control. Nothing here limits any liability that cannot be limited by law. Subject to that, our total liability for a project is limited to the fees you paid us for that project. 10

Changes and cancellation

Either side can end an engagement with reasonable written notice. If work ends early, you pay for the work completed and any non-refundable costs already committed up to that point.

11

Governing law

These terms are governed by the laws of [your country / jurisdiction], and disputes are handled by the courts of [your jurisdiction]. [Complete this with your legal advisor.]

12

Contact

Questions about these terms? Email contactus@theprivilegedcompany.com and we will be glad to talk them through.
