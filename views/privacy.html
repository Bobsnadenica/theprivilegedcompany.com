Privacy Protocol.

Discretion is central to how we work. Here's what we do — and don't do — with your information.

01

How We Handle Your Data

The website itself sets no tracking or advertising cookies and loads no third-party analytics. When you send a brief through our contact form, the details you provide (name, email, phone, and project notes) reach us by email through FormSubmit, a third-party form-processing service, or through your own email client as a fallback. We use that information only to respond to you and scope the work, we do not sell it, and you can ask us to delete it at any time at contactus@theprivilegedcompany.com.

02

Discreet Operations

For our high-net-worth and executive clients, we operate under strict non-disclosure protocols. Your involvement with ThePrivilegedCompany is a private matter. We do not use client names in marketing materials without explicit, written consent.

03

AI Integrity

Our AI-driven engineering processes are executed in isolated environments. We utilize private models to ensure that your proprietary code and data are never leaked into public training sets. Your intellectual property remains yours, exclusively.
